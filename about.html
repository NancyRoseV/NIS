About Us

NDT and Inspection Solutions is a private company that was established and registered in South Africa in 2011 and is situated in the Mpumalanga Highveld , Emalahleni (Witbank). The purpose of the company is to serve the industrial sector by performing inspections that will provide information regarding the quality of equipment, when fabricated / repaired and the integrity when operating. We are committed to delivering a service unparalleled by others and to ensure our clients are satisfied.   Our inspection personnel have years of experience in various industry sectors including power generation, petro-chemical, paper and pulp and more. Their qualifications are proven by continuous training and certification resulting in high quality inspections and client satisfaction.   We will tailor an inspection solution to suit your needs.
