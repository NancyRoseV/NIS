Delivering Excellence in NDT and Inspection Solutions

Services Contact Us

NDT and Inspection Solutions (NIS) specializes in the inspection of equipment that has been placed into service. NIS inspectors are experienced and trained to perform in-service and out-of-service inspections on your equipment to ensure its integrity and to prove that it is still operating at acceptable levels in accordance with the design intent. The inspectors have experience in various industries for example: Petrochemical industry (Oil and Gas), Power Generation (Power Stations), Paper and Pulp and General Engineering. NIS will perform inspection and issue reports including recommended actions / repairs that will ensure the integrity of your equipment is kept at acceptable levels and can continue operating in its current service.
Although the focus of NIS inspections are on the following equipment, inspection could be performed outside of these areas:

- General Nondestructive Testing Methods
- Aboveground Storage Tank inspections
- Pipeline Inspections
- Pressure Vessel Inspections
- API Inspection training
